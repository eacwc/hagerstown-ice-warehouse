Hagerstown ICE Warehouse

No ICE, No Concentration Camps

On February 10, Washington County Commissioners voted unanimously to formally support the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) and ICE's planned detention facility at 16220 Wright Road, Williamsport, Maryland, just outside Hagerstown.

This property - a more than 825,000-square-foot warehouse purchased by DHS for approximately $102.4 million - is believed to be intended as a detention center capable of holding up to 1,500 people.

This is a concentration camp, and it is not welcome in Washington County.
